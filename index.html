Why only html?

It is simple, fast, and dare I say, beautiful. It loosely represents my approach towards design and engineering as well as my developing visual design sense. My corner of the internet, like myself, is permanently in a rapid prototyping stage.
Credit where credit is due, this website is inspired by eladgil.com and motherfuckingwebsite. I also didn't have cellular data plan until college, so I knew the struggle of not have crazy fast wifi to load all the non-functional gimmicks of fancy websites.
