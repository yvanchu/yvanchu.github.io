Who?

Hi, I am Yvan Chu. I like to play with new technology, make things, and work with people. I am currently studying Computer Science @Northwestern, and my goal is to build technology that'll improve the way people live, work, and play.

Vision

Work

Maximizing deep and fulfilling work. Technology will automate the mundane and empower people to reach and stay in flow.
That is why I joined Microsoft. I know work can be incredibly meaningful and fulfilling, but it often is not. I will build technology to help eliminate boring, stressful, and confusing work.

Life

I am actually not sure how living in the future will be or what coherent theme it will entail. I am most hopeful in tech that will help save time and increase safety. For example, self-driving cars.

Play

The current digital ecosystem is polluted with junk content. Like junk food, junk content are entertaining but not fulfilling. It is addicting and mostly driven by engagement and profit. I want to develop experiences that are playful, interactive, and fun. These experiences will help people strengthen existing and develop new relationships.
That is the reason I created BearRoom, but I still have ways to go in order to accomplish my vision in any of these three areas.

Why only html?

It is simple, fast, and dare I say, beautiful. It loosely represents my approach towards design and engineering as well as my developing visual design sense.
Credit where credit is due, this website is inspired by eladgil.com and motherfuckingwebsite. I also didn't have cellular data plan until college, so I knew the struggle of not have crazy fast wifi to load all the non-functional gimmicks of fancy websites.
